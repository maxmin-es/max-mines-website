I'm a 4th-year at Brown University double-majoring in Computer Science and Cognitive Science. I'm interested in the intersection of these two fields, particularly language and music. I currently work in the Language and Thought Lab where we study children learn language. I've spent my past summers working at Sana Labs in Stockholm doing product design for their speech pronunciation product, at Penn Engineering doing NLP research, and at Penn Infant Language Center doing child language acquisition research. I was born and raised outside of Philadelphia, a city which I still very much consider home. I spend most of my free time playing, producing, talking about, or listening to music. I like a lot of different stuff, but currently there are Stevie Wonder, Amy Winehouse, and MGMT posters hanging in my room, and a Feist album in my aux-less car.
